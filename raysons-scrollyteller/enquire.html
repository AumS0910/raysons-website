Before you send

Common questions from OEM buyers.

What casting processes do you offer?+
Shell moulded casting (500 g – 150 kg) and no-bake casting (50 – 500 kg) — both run in-house at our Kolhapur facility.
What materials can you cast?+
Grey CI (IS 210), SG / Ductile (IS 1865), SiMo Ductile and ADI. Every heat is spectrometer-verified before pouring.
Do you machine and export?+
Yes — in-house HMC · VMC · CNC, dimensionally verified and assembly-ready. Already shipped to Italy, the UK, Japan and the USA.
How do I get a quote?+
