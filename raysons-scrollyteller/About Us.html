Era I · Foundation

Foundation of Raysons Industries

The first manufacturer of Resin Coated Sand in Maharashtra. Our foundry materials were inside European machines before we poured our first casting. 40 years in the raw material of precision.

Capacity doubled

A second manufacturing unit — meeting growing demand from OEM foundries across India.

1995

ISO Certification

International quality standard achieved — making Raysons a credible supplier to export-oriented foundries.

Era II · Scale

Specialised bonding & coating

2005

3,000 MT/month plant — ISO certified

Era III · Ignition

450-ton melting capacity commissioned

Shell moulding line operational. ±0.3mm dimensional tolerance. CT8–CT10. First export shipment to a European OEM within 18 months of founding.

Purpose

Our Mission

To design, manufacture and supply precision-engineered shell moulded and no-bake castings with unmatched quality and reliability — through technological excellence, continuous innovation and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To be recognised globally as a trusted and innovative casting solutions partner, setting industry benchmarks in quality, sustainability and customer service.

Our People

The foundry is the team.

Led by ownership with a clear vision: a precision foundry from Kolhapur that global OEM buyers trust for consistency, transparency and engineering discipline.

Our technical team brings metallurgical control, quality leadership and experienced foundry supervision across moulding, melting, pouring, fettling, machining and dispatch.

Certifications & Standards

Standards buyers can verify.

ISO 9001:2015

Every process documented. Every deviation tracked. Audited externally every 12 months.

CT9 / CT10

Casting tolerance grade certified. Dimensional verification against your drawing at despatch.

Surface quality standard for structural castings. Every visible surface classified before shipment.
