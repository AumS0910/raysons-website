Era I · Foundation

Foundation of Raysons Industries

The first manufacturer of Resin Coated Sand (RCS) in Maharashtra — pioneering quality foundry materials in the region.

Expansion with SBR Unit

A second manufacturing unit to meet growing demand, expanding capacity and reach.

1995

ISO Certification

Raysons SBR achieved ISO certification — a commitment to international quality standards.

Era II · Scale

Scaling the sand business.

1997

Inception of SRI Durga Mata

Diversified operations, strengthening our position in foundry and allied industries.

2002

Launch of RR Sand Industries

Founded to support bonding and coating materials — another specialised division.

2005

Raysons Sand Chem Pvt. Ltd.

A state-of-the-art plant with 3,000 tons/month capacity, serving domestic and export markets.

Era III · Ignition

Establishment of Raysons Shell Cast

Founded with a 450-ton monthly melting capacity — our entry into precision shell moulded casting.

No-Bake Foundry Line Commissioned

A modern no-bake line for larger, complex castings — expanding technical capability.
