Precision foundry

Shell moulded and no-bake castings from 0.5 kg to 500 kg.

Raysons Shell Cast is a Shell Moulded and No-Bake foundry producing Cast Iron and Ductile Iron castings from 0.5 kg to 500 kg. Founded within the Raysons Group — a 40-year industrial legacy — we serve the world’s most demanding OEMs across automotive, hydraulics, agriculture, railway, oil & gas, and construction equipment. Our castings have travelled to Italy, UK, Japan, and the United States — made in Kolhapur, trusted globally.

Pillar 01

Shell Moulded
Precision.

In-house resin-coated sand plant. Heated metal patterns. Shells that hold tolerances conventional foundries cannot match. Surface finish that your machinists will thank you for. Parts from 500 grams to 150 kilograms are made in our foundry.

Shell moulding is a precision casting process widely preferred for components requiring superior surface finish, dimensional consistency, and repeatability — compared to conventional green sand moulding.

Superior surface finish
Better dimensional accuracy
Reduced machining requirements
Excellent repeatability for OEM production
Suitable for complex and precision components
Grey Cast Iron SG Iron SiMo Ductile Iron ADI

Pillar 02

No-Bake
Strength.

When your casting weighs 50 kilograms — or 500 — our No-Bake line takes over. Structural components. Complex internal geometries. Heavy-walled housings. The same standard. A bigger scale.

No-bake casting is preferred for medium to large-sized castings where strength, flexibility, and dimensional stability are essential.

Ideal for large and heavy castings
Better dimensional stability
Flexible for complex geometries
Improved mould strength and casting quality
Suitable for customised industrial components

Pillar 03

In-House
Machining.

HMC. VMC. CNC Lathe. On-site. Your casting doesn’t leave our facility until it’s machined, dimensionally verified, and ready for your assembly line. One partner. One responsibility. Zero gaps.

Our Process

From raw material to
finished casting.

01

Raw Material Inspection

Careful inspection and testing of incoming raw materials to ensure consistent casting quality from the very beginning.

02

Sand Preparation & Shell Formation

Controlled sand properties and precise shell formation for superior mould strength and dimensional accuracy.

03

Melting & Metallurgical Control

Monitored melting practices and metallurgical control to achieve the required chemical and mechanical properties.

04

Precision Pouring Process

Optimised pouring parameters and process discipline to ensure defect-free and reliable castings.

05

Fettling & Surface Finishing

Controlled fettling and finishing operations for clean surfaces, improved appearance, and dimensional consistency.

06

Machining, Inspection & Dispatch

Final machining, quality inspection, and dispatch carried out to meet customer specifications and delivery requirements.
