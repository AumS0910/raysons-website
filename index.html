Shell moulded and no-bake castings from 0.5 kg to 500 kg.

Raysons Shell Cast is a Shell Moulded and No-Bake foundry producing Cast Iron and Ductile Iron castings from 0.5 kg to 500 kg. Founded within the Raysons Group — a 40-year industrial legacy — we serve the world’s most demanding OEMs across automotive, hydraulics, agriculture, railway, oil & gas, and construction equipment. Our castings have travelled to Italy, UK, Japan, and the United States — made in Kolhapur, trusted globally.
