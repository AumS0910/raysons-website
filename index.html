Our Process

From raw material to
finished casting.

Sand Mixing

Precision-grade silica sand coated with thermosetting resin binder.

Shell Making

Heated pattern plates form precision shell mould halves.

Metal Melting

Induction furnace melting with spectrometer-verified chemistry.

Pouring

Controlled molten metal pour into assembled shell moulds.

Shakeout

Clean shell removal revealing the near-net-shape casting.

Finishing

Shot blasting, heat treatment, machining, and QC inspection.
