About Us

Part of Something Bigger

  • Raysons Group founded in the 1980s in Kolhapur.
  • Diversified industrial group across Foundry, Real Estate, Hospitality, Retail, and Renewable Energy.
  • Four group companies: Raysons Shell Cast · Raysons Sandchem Pvt Ltd · Raysons Industries · Raysons Marketing Pvt Ltd.
  • Partnered brands: REGENTA Place · Westside · Neora · Sky Zone.
  • Raysons Shell Cast established in 2021 as the group’s precision manufacturing vertical.
  • Strong foundation in Resin Coated Sand and casting technologies.

Purpose

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To design, manufacture, and supply precision-engineered shell moulded and no-bake castings with unmatched quality and reliability — delivering value through technological excellence, continuous innovation, and sustainable practices.

Our Vision

To be recognised globally as a trusted and innovative casting solutions partner, setting industry benchmarks in quality, sustainability, and customer service — driven by advanced technology, integrity, and a passion for excellence.

Our Values

  • Innovation — Continuously adopting new technologies and smarter processes.
  • Quality — Ensuring zero-defect manufacturing and superior performance.
  • Sustainability — Promoting an eco-friendly and safe workplace.
  • Integrity — Building transparent, long-term relationships with customers and partners.
  • Teamwork — Empowering people to grow and achieve excellence together.

Our Journey

Growth & Innovation
since 1987.

1987

Foundation of Raysons Industries

Raysons Industries was established, becoming the first manufacturer of Resin Coated Sand (RCS) in Maharashtra — pioneering a new era of quality foundry materials in the region.

1992

Expansion with SBR Unit

To meet growing demand, we set up our second manufacturing unit under SBR, expanding production capacity and customer reach.

1995

ISO Certification

Raysons SBR achieved ISO certification — marking our commitment to international quality standards and consistent process excellence.

1997

Inception of SRI Durga Mata

We diversified operations by establishing SRI Durga Mata, further strengthening our position in the foundry and allied industries.

2002

Launch of RR Sand Industries

Founded RR Sand Industries to support growing requirements for bonding and coating materials — adding another specialised division under the Raysons Group umbrella.

2005

Raysons Sand Chem Pvt. Ltd.

Commissioned a state-of-the-art manufacturing plant with 3,000 tons/month capacity, enabling us to serve larger domestic and export markets.

2021

Establishment of Raysons Shell Cast

Raysons Shell Cast was founded with a 450-ton monthly melting capacity — marking our entry into precision shell moulded casting manufacturing for leading global industries.

2022

No-Bake Foundry Line Commissioned

To meet growing demand for larger and complex castings, we commissioned a modern No-Bake foundry line — expanding technical capabilities and production flexibility.

2026

Advanced Machine Shop & Heat Treatment

A dedicated CNC/VMC machine shop and in-house heat treatment facility — enabling fully machined, ready-to-assemble casting deliveries.

2027

New HMC & VMC Machine Additions

Further expansion of machining capabilities with additional HMC and VMC machines — cementing Raysons as a full-service precision casting partner.

Our People

The Foundry is the Team

Raysons Shell Cast is led by ownership with a clear vision: to build a precision foundry from Kolhapur that global OEM buyers can trust for consistency, transparency, and engineering discipline.

Our technical team brings together metallurgical control, quality leadership, and experienced foundry supervision across moulding, melting, pouring, fettling, machining, and dispatch.

Operators are trained through process discipline, shop-floor supervision, inspection feedback, and repeatable work standards so every casting is handled with accountability from raw material to shipment.
