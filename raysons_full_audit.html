CRITICAL
Stats counters stuck at 0 on all viewports
All 4 stats (0MT, 0+, 0kg, 0) never animate. The IntersectionObserver threshold is too high — on mobile and some desktop zoom levels the stats section never fully enters the viewport at 30% threshold.
Fix: lower threshold to 0.1, add rootMargin "0px 0px -60px 0px", verify data-target attributes exist on .stats__item elements
CRITICAL
Mobile canvas 112vw causes horizontal overflow scroll
At ≤768px the .hero__canvas-wrap is set to 112vw. This makes the entire body horizontally scrollable on every phone. Users can swipe right to see a black gap — breaks the illusion immediately.
Fix: change to 100vw, add overflow-x:hidden to .hero and .hero__stage
CRITICAL
Two competing hero headlines visible simultaneously on mobile
"WE ARE PRECISION." (left) and "WE ARE THE PART INSIDE THE MACHINE." (right) both render at the same time on 430px screens. They overlap the canvas and each other. Neither is readable. The right headline's large font size pushes layout at narrow widths.
Fix: hide .hero__right on mobile (display:none at ≤768px), let left headline be the sole identity
HIGH
shadowBlur on 60 ember particles — main Android lag cause
Every ember calls ctx.shadowBlur = sz*3 per frame. 60 embers × 60fps = 3,600 GPU blur passes/second. Canvas2D shadowBlur is software-rendered on Android Chrome — this alone drops Pixel/OnePlus to ~20fps during scroll.
Fix: remove shadowBlur entirely, fake the glow with a second larger semi-transparent circle (2 fillArc calls, zero blur)
HIGH
Canvas DPR rendering at 2× on Android (3× physical screens)
Pixel 9 / OnePlus 13 have device pixel ratios of 2.75–3.5. Even capped at 2, canvas renders at ~2160×4680 physical pixels per frame on a Pixel 9. That is an enormous canvas fill operation 60 times per second.
Fix: cap DPR at 1.5 on mobile — Math.min(devicePixelRatio, isMobile() ? 1.5 : 2)
HIGH
Float CTA (Request a Quote pill) hidden on mobile
display:none at ≤768px. This is the primary conversion button on the entire site. Mobile users — which are likely the majority of visitors — have no persistent CTA visible while scrolling. They have to find the enquiry section at the very bottom.
Fix: remove display:none, shrink to font-size:.6rem, padding:.5rem .9rem on mobile — keep it visible
HIGH
Stats column layout — 1 column on mobile wastes enormous scroll space
4 stats stacked vertically in a single column takes ~600px of height on mobile. A 2×2 grid would take ~280px. This forces users to scroll through a huge empty stats section before reaching real content.
Fix: grid-template-columns: repeat(2,1fr) at ≤768px. Keep the drawn gradient line above.
HIGH
Process right panel (sticky visual) hidden on mobile
display:none on the process__right at ≤1100px. The process section becomes a plain numbered text list with no visual. On desktop the ghost number and contextual image make this section feel premium — on mobile it reads like a plain HTML list.
Fix: show a simplified version below the timeline on mobile — small 16:9 image + ghost number, display:block at full width
HIGH
Hero scroll travel 220vh on mobile — feels endless
The hero section takes 220vh to scroll through on mobile (equivalent to scrolling 2.2 full phone screens just for the hero). Before users reach About, stats, or any product info they've scrolled through a huge canvas sequence. Bounce risk is high.
Fix: reduce to 160vh at ≤768px. Enough for the 240-frame sequence without feeling punishing.
MEDIUM
Beats section schematic barely visible on mobile (0.42 opacity, 112vw)
The engineering schematic is 112vw wide at 42% opacity on mobile — an unreadable blurred texture behind the beat text. It contributes no visual value and costs GPU resources.
Fix: display:none the schematic at ≤768px entirely. The beat text is the hero on mobile — let it breathe.
MEDIUM
body::before CSS variable repaint — 60 times/second
--atmo-warmth and --atmo-opacity update on every rAF tick via updateCinematicPage(). body::before is position:fixed; inset:0 — the browser repaints the full viewport background 60×/sec. On Android this causes consistent frame drops.
Fix: throttle CSS var updates to every 6th frame. Values change slowly — user cannot perceive the difference.
MEDIUM
Process section images have spaces in filenames
Image paths like /images/pillar 1.jpeg, /images/pilllar 2.jpeg (note triple-l typo) contain spaces and typos. Spaces in URLs can cause 404s on certain servers/CDNs even if they work on Vercel. The triple-l typo will likely 404.
Fix: rename files to pillar-1.jpg through pillar-6.jpg, update all src attributes. Also fix "pilllar" typo to "pillar".
MEDIUM
FORGED, PRECISION, QC decorative text — orphaned and purposeless
Three large decorative text elements (FORGED in hero, PRECISION between hero and stats, QC before stats) appear as floating orphaned words. They have no context labels, no animation trigger, and no relationship to surrounding content. They confuse rather than impress.
Fix: remove FORGED and QC entirely. Keep PRECISION only if it becomes part of a coherent ambient text system with consistent treatment.
MEDIUM
Capabilities grid collapses to 1 column on mobile
4 capabilities columns at single column = 4 full-width cards with 240px+ height each. The section becomes extremely long to scroll through. Ghost numbers (01-04) lose their visual context at 1-col.
Fix: 2×2 grid at ≤768px instead of 1 column. Reduces section height by ~50%.
LOW
Logo uses WhatsApp image filename in production
The nav logo src is WhatsApp_Image_2026-05-14_at_10.22.47_PM-removebg-preview.png — a raw WhatsApp export filename. Unprofessional if someone inspects the page source, and fragile naming.
Fix: rename to raysons-logo.png or logo.png
LOW
Privacy Policy and Terms of Service links in footer go nowhere
Footer Legal column has Privacy Policy and Terms of Service links. These are presumably # links or missing pages. For a B2B site targeting international OEMs (Italy, UK, Japan, USA mentioned in copy) these need to exist.
Fix: either create minimal policy pages or remove the Legal footer column entirely until policies are ready.
LOW
No Open Graph / social meta tags
When someone shares the URL on WhatsApp, LinkedIn, or email — which is how B2B leads will share this — there's no og:image, og:title beyond the page title, or og:description. The link will appear as plain text with no preview.
Fix: add og:title, og:description, og:image (use a frame from the sequence or a product photo), og:url, twitter:card meta tags in the HTML head.
LOW
Enquiry CTA links to mailto — no form tracking
The primary CTA "Request a Quote" links directly to mailto:info@raysonsgroup.com. There's no form submission, no lead capture, no analytics event. You have no way to know how many people clicked the CTA.
Fix: add a simple inline form or at minimum a JS click event that fires a Google Analytics / GTM event when the mailto link is clicked.

UX & Content Issues
HIGH
No persistent mobile CTA — zero conversion path on phones
The float CTA is hidden on mobile. The only CTA buttons are inside the beats section (scroll-triggered) and at the very bottom of the page. A B2B buyer on a phone who wants to enquire has to scroll to the very end to find a contact button.
Restore float CTA on mobile. It's the single highest-impact conversion fix.
HIGH
Enquiry is a mailto link — no form, no tracking
Clicking "Request a Quote" opens the email app. On Android/iOS this often fails silently if no email client is configured. You also have no way to track how many leads came from the website.
Add a minimal 3-field form (Name, Company, Message) with a POST to Formspree or Netlify Forms — takes 30 minutes and gives you a lead capture pipeline.
MEDIUM
Pillar labels (Pillar 01–04) in beats section — confusing terminology
The beats section eyebrows say "Pillar 01", "Pillar 02" etc. This is internal organizational language — it means nothing to a buyer. Replace with descriptive labels like "SHELL MOULDING", "NO-BAKE CASTING", "IN-HOUSE MACHINING", "EXPORT QUALITY".
Replace all beat__eyebrow "Pillar 0X" text with the actual product/capability name.
MEDIUM
Phone number missing from entire site
The contact section has email and location but no phone number. For a B2B foundry serving international OEMs, a direct phone number (especially WhatsApp for Indian business) is expected and builds trust.
Add phone number to CTA contact block and footer. Format: +91 XXXXX XXXXX with a WhatsApp link.
LOW
No sitemap.xml or robots.txt
Basic SEO infrastructure is missing. A foundry targeting international OEMs needs to be discoverable via Google for "shell moulded castings manufacturer India" type searches.
Add /sitemap.xml and /robots.txt. Takes 10 minutes on Vercel.

1. Fix stats counters main.js
In initObservers(), change the statsObs threshold from .3 to .1 and add rootMargin: '0px 0px -60px 0px'. Also verify every .stats__item has a valid data-target integer attribute in the HTML.
2. Fix canvas horizontal overflow on mobile style.css
Change .hero__canvas-wrap at ≤768px from width:112vw to width:100vw. Add overflow-x:hidden to both .hero and .hero__stage. Also add overflow-x:hidden to body as a safety net.
3. Hide hero right panel on mobile style.css
Add .hero__right { display:none } inside the @media(max-width:768px) block. The left headline is sufficient identity on mobile. The right one creates visual chaos.
⚠️ High — Fix this week
4. Remove shadowBlur from embers main.js
In Ember.draw() remove all ctx.shadowBlur and ctx.shadowColor lines. Replace with a second ctx.arc() at sz*3 radius with opacity * 0.18 — fakes the glow at zero GPU cost. This is the #1 Android performance fix.
5. Cap DPR at 1.5 on mobile main.js
In sizeCanvases() change DPR calculation to: const dpr = Math.min(window.devicePixelRatio||1, isMobile() ? 1.5 : 2). Reduces canvas pixel area by 44% on Pixel/OnePlus without visible quality loss.
6. Restore float CTA on mobile style.css
Remove .float-cta { display:none } from the 768px media query. Add instead: font-size:.6rem; padding:.5rem .85rem .5rem 1.1rem; bottom:.6rem. Keep it visible — it's your only persistent conversion trigger on mobile.
7. Stats 2×2 grid on mobile style.css
In the 768px media query change .stats__inner { grid-template-columns: 1fr } to grid-template-columns: repeat(2,1fr); gap: 1.5rem. Cuts the stats section height in half on mobile.
8. Throttle CSS variable updates main.js
Add a frame counter to updateCinematicPage(). Only update --forge-heat, --atmo-opacity, --atmo-warmth on every 6th frame. These drive body::before full-viewport repaints — throttling from 60/sec to 10/sec eliminates a major Android paint cost.
9. Disable Three.js on mobile threeScene.js
Wrap the entire Three.js init in if (window.innerWidth >= 768). Two GPU contexts (WebGL + Canvas2D) on a mobile GPU compete for the same command buffer. Killing WebGL on mobile frees ~2.5ms/frame.
10. Reduce hero height on mobile style.css
Change .hero { height: 220vh } at ≤768px to height: 160vh; min-height: 1050px. Still plays all 240 frames. Stops the hero feeling like an endless void before users reach real content.
