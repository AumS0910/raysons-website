Questions & Answers

Common questions
from OEM buyers.

We offer Shell Moulded Casting (500 gm – 150 kg) and No-Bake Casting (50 kg – 500 kg). Shell moulding uses resin-coated sand on heated metal patterns for superior surface finish and tight tolerances. No-Bake handles larger, complex structural components. Both processes are run in-house at our Kolhapur facility.

We cast Grey Cast Iron (IS210), SG / Ductile Iron (IS1865), SiMo Ductile Iron, and ADI (Austempered Ductile Iron). Every heat is spectrometer-verified (Bruker Q2 ION) before pouring to confirm chemistry compliance.

Current capacity is 500 MT per year across shell moulding and no-bake lines. We are actively expanding to 700 MT. Our 500 kg medium-frequency induction furnace allows precise temperature control and consistent metallurgical output.

Yes. We have in-house HMC, VMC, and CNC Lathe capability. Your casting does not leave our facility until it is machined, dimensionally verified, and ready for your assembly line. One partner. Zero gaps.

We are ISO 9001:2015 certified. Our castings are produced and tested to ASTM A802 (visual inspection), JDM B15, JDT 17015, and other customer-specific standards. Full QC documentation including spectro reports, hardness certificates, and mechanical test results are provided per shipment.

Yes, we are export-ready. Our castings have been supplied to customers in Italy, the United Kingdom, Japan, and the United States. We have experience with export packing, documentation, and compliance with international shipping requirements.

Lead times depend on casting complexity and volume. Typically 4–8 weeks for new parts (including pattern/tooling), and 2–4 weeks for repeat orders. We respond to all RFQs within 48 hours of receiving drawings and specifications.

Send us your drawing (PDF or DXF), material grade, weight range, and annual requirement to industries@raysonsgroup.com. Our engineering team will respond within 48 hours with a detailed quotation.
